— About

Sandesh Pandey is a Kathmandu-based brand strategist and creator whose work sits at the intersection of brand, content, and culture. He runs marketing for early-stage companies, scales creator-led platforms, and builds ventures rooted in Nepal's identity.

He is presently Head of Marketing at FinTruction, Brand Manager at Unchained, founder of Kranti — a content platform on current affairs and culture — and co-founder of Stitch, a cultural fashion brand.

Outside of his commercial work, he is a Uunchai Fellow, a researcher with Project Abhaya, and a former Event Manager at the SXC Literary Club. He studied science at St. Xavier's College, Maitighar.
