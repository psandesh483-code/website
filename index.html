01 / about

A bit about me.

I’m a Kathmandu-based brand strategist and creator. Most of my work sits where brand, content, and culture meet — running marketing for early-stage companies, scaling creator-led platforms, and building ventures rooted in Nepal’s identity.

Right now I’m Head of Marketing at FinTruction, Brand Manager at Unchained, founder of Kranti — a content platform on current affairs and culture — and co-founder of Stitch, a cultural fashion brand.

Outside of work I’m a Uunchai Fellow, a researcher with Project Abhaya, and a former Event Manager at the SXC Literary Club. I studied Science at St. Xavier’s College, Maitighar.
