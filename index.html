01 · About

A bit about me

I'm a marketer who cares less about vanity metrics and more about the ones that move revenue. I help brands find their position, sharpen their story, and turn attention into growth, the kind of marketing that actually works.

Right now I'm Head of Marketing at FinTruction, a construction-accounting firm for U.S. contractors, while running Kranti, a content platform on current affairs and culture. Before that I was Brand Manager at Unchained, a creator agency, through late 2025. My work runs across strategy, content, brand, and growth, with a soft spot for B2B. I studied science at St. Xavier's College, Maitighar.

I turn slow ideas into things people remember.
